The Y2K aesthetic isn't slowing down — it's evolving. In 2026, phone cases are
louder, cuter and more *personal* than ever. Whether you're chasing that
holographic shimmer or building a charm-stacked masterpiece, here's everything
trending right now.

## 1. Holographic & aura finishes

The single biggest look of the year is the **holographic aura** — cases that
shift color as they catch the light. They pair perfectly with a minimalist
home screen and make every mirror selfie pop. Browse the
[full collection](/products) to find your shade.

## 2. Character charms are the main event

A case is no longer just a case — it's a base layer. The move in 2026 is
**stacking charms**: a beaded phone strap, a dangling mascot, a tiny mirror.
Our [Case + Grip + Charm bundles](/devices/iphone) let you build the whole look
in one tap.

## 3. Sanrio & friends, forever

Hello Kitty, Kuromi, My Melody and Cinnamoroll continue to rule the kawaii
world. If you're new to the trend, start with our
[Sanrio collection](/collections/sanrio) — it's the easiest way to add a hit of
nostalgia to your everyday carry.

## 4. Function meets cute

The best part of 2026? You don't have to choose between adorable and protective.
Look for:

- **MagSafe compatibility** so charging and accessories just work
- **Raised edges** around the screen and camera
- **Shock-absorbing builds** that survive real life

## How to choose your case

Think about three things: your **device**, your **daily vibe**, and how much
**personality** you want to add. Start with a base case you love, then layer in
a grip and a charm to make it unmistakably *yours*.
