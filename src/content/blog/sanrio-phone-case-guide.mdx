Few things say *kawaii* like a Sanrio phone case. But with so many characters,
which one is your match? Here's a friendly guide to the icons — and where to find
their cases.

## Hello Kitty — the timeless classic

The original superstar. Hello Kitty cases lean sweet, clean and endlessly
giftable. Perfect if you love a **red-and-white**, polished look that never goes
out of style.

## Kuromi — the cute rebel

Mischievous, a little goth, all attitude. Kuromi cases bring **purple, black and
punk-pastel** energy — ideal if your aesthetic has an edge.

## My Melody — soft and dreamy

Pink, gentle and full of cottagecore charm. My Melody is the go-to for a
**soft-girl** palette and cozy vibes.

## Cinnamoroll — the fluffy sky pup

Pastel blue, fluffy and impossibly serene. Cinnamoroll cases are for anyone who
wants **calm, airy** cuteness.

## How to pick your character

Ask yourself:

- What's your **color palette** — warm and classic, or cool and edgy?
- Do you want **sweet**, **punk**, **dreamy**, or **calm**?
- Will you **stack charms** on top? (Always yes.)

Once you know your vibe, the rest is easy.
